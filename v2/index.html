Doug Aitken is a man of many sides.
He works in a support team in the Dundee customer contact centre of a large multi-channel retailer. He occasionally builds simple websites for people, small businesses, and groups.

He is on twitter under @dougaitken, all music which can be, is scrobbled to his last.fm, he uploads iPhone photos to instagram, and is a long time SU2 tracking his check-ins on Foursquare.

He's a great mix of support, technical, analytical, and banter.

He's certainly not the installation artist.
